About Us

Malganga Sea Foods

Malganga Sea Foods is a family-owned business with over 40 years of experience in the seafood industry. Starting at Sassoon Dock in Colaba, Mumbai, they aimed to provide the freshest seafood to Mumbai residents. Now in their second generation, they serve clients across India, offering top-quality seafood products. Their growth from a single dock to a nationwide network highlights their commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. They have expanded beyond Mumbai, establishing a strong presence in Pune and reaching clients nationwide. Today, they supply a wide range of fresh and frozen seafood to restaurants, hotels, caterers, and retail chains across India.

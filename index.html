Our Products

Malganga Sea Foods

Malganga Sea Foods is a family-owned business with over 40 years of experience in the seafood industry. Starting at Sassoon Dock in Colaba, Mumbai, they aimed to provide the freshest seafood to Mumbai residents. Now in their second generation, they serve clients across India, offering top-quality seafood products. Their growth from a single dock to a nationwide network highlights their commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. They have expanded beyond Mumbai, establishing a strong presence in Pune and reaching clients nationwide. Today, they supply a wide range of fresh and frozen seafood to restaurants, hotels, caterers, and retail chains across India.

Nationwide Reach

Nationwide Reach with Local Roots at Malganga Sea Foods highlights their extensive distribution network that spans across India, while maintaining their origins in Mumbai. Their seamless supply chain ensures swift and optimal delivery of seafood to top hotels, restaurants, and retail chains nationwide. Proud of their journey, they continue to offer the finest seafood, catering to restaurants, caterers, and seafood enthusiasts across the country.

Trust and Excellence

A Legacy of Trust and Excellence at Malganga Sea Foods is built on enduring values and a strong family legacy. Now in the hands of the second generation, they continue to uphold the founders’ principles of transparency, trust, and quality in every transaction, reinforcing their reputation as a reliable seafood supplier.

Commitment

Our Commitment to Quality at Malganga Sea Foods emphasizes sourcing the freshest catch and maintaining strict quality controls. They offer a wide range of seafood, including fish, prawns, lobsters, crabs, and exotic varieties, in both fresh and frozen formats. Their efficient sourcing and distribution network ensure prompt delivery of premium seafood across India.
